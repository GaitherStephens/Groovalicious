Groovalicious iOS widget is sharp, elegant, and a crafted masterpiece.

FEATURES:

Dark and Light versions
Custom Opacity
Custom Text Colors
12 & 24 hour modes
Multi-language support(en, de, fr, it, nl, pl, pt, ru, sp)
System Monitor
Weather Temperature & Current Condition
Compatible with XenHTML & iWidgets
